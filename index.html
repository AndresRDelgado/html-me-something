The History of Folly in Modern America

It's really silly

But not so silly as to make you laugh.

Magazines

There have been a bunch of good ones, but it started with the Harvard Lampoon and culminated with Mad Magazine in the 1960s.

Harvard Lampoon

This was put together by a bunch of kids with bright futures and an impulse to ridicule.

Cracked

This was an unfunny version of Mad Magazine, but somehow it has taken its place as the foremost national humor publication - certainly online.

Mad

The usual gang of idiots were a bunch of guys who could have excelled in many fields but preferred to loaf around doodling for a terrible magazine.
